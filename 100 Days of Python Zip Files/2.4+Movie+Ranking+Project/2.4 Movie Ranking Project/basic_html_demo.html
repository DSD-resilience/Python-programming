The Best Movies According to John

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Commitments

This is a musical featuring an Irish soul band. Sexy, funny, speaks to me.

Godfather I and II

Difficult for me to separate, provides lessons in power and loss.

2001: A Space Odyssey

Super beautiful film, rejuvenated the genre. Really artistic. Attempts to convey feeling in the same way as music.
